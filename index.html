The Cornell Crime Logs

Like many universities (examples), Cornell University keeps a public log of incidents reported to the Cornell University Police Department. The log is published almost daily, but few people know about it. As of today, Nicholas Bogel-Borroughs remains the only person to be tweeting from the hashtag #CornellCrimeLog. This visualization is an attempt to make this information more accessible to students and faculty.

Note: The Cornell Crime Logs are erased past two months from the current date. For this reason, there is only data from about two months ago as of today. If you are interested in contributing to the dataset, please contact me at beatricezjin (at) gmail (dot) com, or visit the Github page for this project.
